OUR STORY

Across Africa, many women are leading change in their communities, and contributing to solving Africa's numerous challenges—teaching in rural schools, running health awareness campaings, advocating for gender equality, and driving social innovation. But then we want to equip them, as well as all that'll help them do their work, to be able to do more or scale their impact digitally without facing physical constraints.

Despite having a burning passion to make a difference, without having the platform and the skills to lead and reach people globally, it becomes difficult to mobilize people, raise funds, or expand impact.

SheInternet was born out of the need to bridge this gap. We saw how women working in the social sector—weather through NGO's, nonprofits, or grassroots initiatives or even small scale innovators—struggled to scale their efforts due to digital barriers. Inspired by their resillience, we set out to provide them with the tools they need to thrive. In our supports, we will equip these women with the knowledge, skills and confidence to tell their stories, build their influence, and be global leaders through digitals training, mentorship, and networking.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower African women with access to the internet and transformational knowledge that fuels their potential growth, equipping them with the skills, confidence and resources to lead, advocate, and innovate.

Our Vision

To empower women across Africa by providng internet access and skill, enhancing them to launch 100+ social impact projects, drive policy change, and actively reduce Africa's challenges by 40% through digital innovation, advocacy and collaboration.
