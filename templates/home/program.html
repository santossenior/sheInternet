2025 Data Support

The Data Support Program provides internet access to women who are leading change or aspiring to do so—whether they are women passionate about leading change, educator. nonprofit workers, health advocate, grassroots leaders, or women passionate about launching new solutions. This program ensures they the connectivity needed to:

  • Connect with donors, partners, and beneficiaries.
  • Access online learning and resources for personal and leadership growth.
  • Promote and scale their initiatives using digital platforms.
  • Research, develop, and launch innovative community solutions.
  • Stay informed and engaged with the global community.

Who Can Apply

The Data Support Program is designed for women who are actively driving changes or have a strong desire to start impactful initiative. You can apply if you are:

  • NonProfit & NGO Workers: Women working or volunteering in NGOs, charities, or social impact organizations who need internet access for advocacy, fundraising, and outreach.
  • Community Leaders & Advocates: Women Championing causes such as gender equality, education, health, climate action, or social justice.
  • Social Entrepreneurs & Innovators: Women developing grassroots solution to communities challenges and seeking online resources to grow.
  • Aspiring Change—Makers: Women who have vision for impact-driven work but lack internet access to research, plan, or start their initiatives.
  • Grass Root Organisers: Women moblizing communities for local initiatives, policy change, or social impact.
  • Rural Educators & Trainers: Women teaching in undeserved areas or leading training programs and workshops.
  • Health Awareness Campaigners: Women running initiatives in maternal health, mental health, public health awareness, or disease Prevention.
  • Remote Workers in the Social Sectors: Women working remotely for NGOs, nonprofits or social enterprises who require stable internet for their work.

Application closes: April 30, 2025
